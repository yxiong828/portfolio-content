About the website

A recipe is full of quantities, measurements, and instructions, but it is not just a formula. Often, it is a memory—of that week you were in Florence, or of your mom baking cookies, or of your homeland across the world. Which is to say: Often, it is personal. Discover original recipes curated by world-class chefs for a variety of cuisines, including Chinese Dumplings, French Mushroom Pie, Japanese Sushi, Indian Fried Rice, Taiwan Pork Rice, Spanish Paella, Thai Curry Bowl, Korean Bibimbap, German Suassage with Sauerkraut...

Tasting the World this here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy. We offer recipes and cooking advice for home cooks, by home cooks. Helping create “kitchen wins” is what we’re all about.
